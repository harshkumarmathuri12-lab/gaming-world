How to Play Snake Game

The Snake Game is a classic arcade game where you control a snake that grows longer as it eats food. Use arrow keys to move the snake and avoid hitting the walls or yourself.

Controls

  • ⬆️ Up Arrow – Move Up
  • ⬇️ Down Arrow – Move Down
  • ⬅️ Left Arrow – Move Left
  • ➡️ Right Arrow – Move Right

Game Tips

Try to stay in the center of the board and avoid quick turns. Plan your moves ahead as the snake grows longer.
